About

CASASONEH is a Perth-based architecture & interior design studio focused on calm, enduring and highly functional spaces. We collaborate closely with clients to craft thoughtful homes and boutique commercial environments with refined detailing, natural light and balanced material palettes.

Our process is simple: listen deeply, design precisely, and deliver with clarity. From concept to completion, we seek timeless outcomes that feel considered, warm and effortless to live in.
