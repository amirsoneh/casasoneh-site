About

DEZOSONEH is a Perth-based, design-led architecture & interior design studio shaping calm, enduring and highly functional spaces. While the brand is new, the practice is not — we bring over 18 years of experience across residential, hospitality and boutique commercial projects on three continents.

We collaborate closely with clients to craft thoughtful homes and distinctive commercial environments with refined detailing, natural light and balanced material palettes.

Our process is simple: listen deeply, design precisely, and deliver with clarity. From concept to completion, we seek timeless outcomes that feel considered, warm and effortless to live in.
